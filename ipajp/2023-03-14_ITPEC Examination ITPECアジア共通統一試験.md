## 概要
ITPEC Examination is a common IT examination conducted in the Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam, Myanmar, Mongolia, and Bangladesh. It enables to verify the competence of IT engineer with common criteria in the Asian region.

The exam system is based on ITEE  that is administered by IPA, Japan. 
People who pass the FE or AP exam are eligible for preferential immigration treatment for residency status in Japan.

All ITPEC member countries cooperate with each other to implement and promote ITPEC Examination, while IPA contributes to the supports of formulating exam questions, promoting the examination and the other related activities.

For further information:
