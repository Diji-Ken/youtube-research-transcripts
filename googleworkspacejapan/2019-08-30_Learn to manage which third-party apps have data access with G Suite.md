## 概要
You can control which third-party apps can access your data using G Suite’s API permission tool. In this installment of the Google Cloud Security Showcase, you’ll learn how the tool works, and how to either trust certain access or limit their access to G Suite services. You can also see which third-party apps you may have granted access without realizing it, and how to block those apps to limit your exposure.
