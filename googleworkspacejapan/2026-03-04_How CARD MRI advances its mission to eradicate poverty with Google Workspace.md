## 概要
Featured in this video: Marilyn Magampon-Manila, Creative Ideas Director, CARD MRI 

Executive summary: CARD MRI is a group of development organizations in the Philippines providing financial, health, insurance, and education services to marginalized communities, with a long-term mission of poverty eradication and nation building. For CARD MRI, technology is not an end in itself, but a tool to help teams work more efficiently so they can focus on serving clients and improving lives.

Before adopting Google Workspace, CARD MRI’s teams worked across multiple disconnected platforms, creating version confusion, email overload, and slow approval cycles. By moving to Google Workspace, the organization unified its workflows into a single, integrated environment using Docs, Gmail, Calendar, and other tools for real-time collaboration. This shift dramatically improved turnaround times for content review and approvals—from up to a week down to 24 hours or less—while also supporting a younger, more agile workforce that thrives on quick feedback and collaboration. Looking ahead, CARD MRI is embracing Gemini as a supportive AI assistant to help teams work faster and more efficiently, allowing them to spend less time on paperwork and more time advancing their mission to lift communities out of poverty.

Challenge: CARD MRI needed to support a growing, digitally native workforce while managing complex creative, editorial, and approval workflows across multiple teams. Fragmented tools, duplicated documents, and email-heavy processes slowed work and diverted time away from client-focused activities central to the organization’s mission.


Solution: CARD MRI adopted Google Workspace to bring collaboration, communication, and document management into a single, integrated platform. Real-time editing in Docs, centralized file access, and automatic scheduling through Calendar streamlined daily work and eliminated version confusion. As the organization looks to the future, Gemini is being introduced as an assistive tool to enhance creativity, reduce manual effort, and improve efficiency—without replacing the human-centered work that defines CARD MRI’s mission.

Results: With Google Workspace, CARD MRI has significantly accelerated how teams collaborate and deliver work. Editorial and approval turnaround times have been reduced from days to under 24 hours, meetings are easier to manage with automatic calendar integration, and teams are able to deliver more than expected through faster feedback loops. By reducing administrative friction and simplifying paperwork, CARD MRI’s staff can focus more fully on serving clients and advancing their goal of poverty eradication. Technology, supported by Google Workspace and Gemini, has become a true enabler—helping CARD MRI work smarter, move faster, and stay centered on its mission of creating a life out of poverty for the communities it serves.
