## 概要
Since launching on cellphones in 2008, Android has undergone a number of changes and improvements, becoming the world’s most used mobile operating system, with more than 1.4 billion users globally.
 
One aspect that has never changed, however, is our ongoing commitment to keeping Android secure.
 
Our soon to be released report, Android Security 2015 Year in Review, details how we are protecting Android devices and data today. To discuss the results of this report in-depth, we’re hosting a Hangout on Air on April 19, 2016 at 10am PST with Adrian Ludwig – Lead Engineer for Android Security.
