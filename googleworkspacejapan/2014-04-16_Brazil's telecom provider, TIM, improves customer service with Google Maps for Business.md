## 概要
To improve the quality their service, TIM Cellular developed a three-year plan to better communicate with its more than 70 million customers. Using Google Maps, TIM Cellular can now show the location of all its antennas and WiFi Hotspots, and display where there is 2G, 3G or 4G coverage.
