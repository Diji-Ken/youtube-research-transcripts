G Suite is a low-risk, high reward entry point to bring your organization up to speed in the cloud age. How do you upgrade your environment while also providing your users with stellar tools that they will adopt to be more productive? This panel will create a space that offers insight into Adler Planetarium’s migration to Google, complete with several different perspectives of those who played important roles throughout the transition.
