## 概要
On 17 May 2012, Google officially launched its enterprise business in Taiwan at the Atmosphere on Tour event in Taipei, at Taipei 10. Nearly 300 executives spent the afternoon learning about business transformation enabled by technology, and networking with their peers and colleagues.
