## 概要
Managing projects and getting things done can be difficult when the tools we use don't fit our needs. You want tools that help accelerate projects and reduce friction. Mark Ridley, Director of Technology for Reed Online will join us for a Hangout on Air to discuss how his team uses Google Docs, Drive and Hangouts to run the UK's #1 job site, reed.co.uk.
