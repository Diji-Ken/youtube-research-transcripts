## 概要
Part of the Google Apps for Education Professional Development webinar series.


Skill level: Beginner
Lead by Wendy Gorton, Google Apps for Education Certified Trainer and Google Certified Teacher

The Google Science Fair (www.google.com/sciencefair) is here, and along with it a slick interface with all of Google's trappings to help your students document and share their research with the world and have a chance to win some fantastic prizes. Students can enter from January 11th-April 4th, and in this session, we'll delve into how you can teach your students to use Google Apps to get the job done, from using Search, Scholar, and Alerts to start your research, Docs to collect data and collaborate with partners, Google Videos to document it all, and Google Sites to post a beautiful collection of your work. It's a fantastic, authentic way to get your kids-- and you!-- comfortable with Google Apps.

Note: Google Science Fair is only open to students ages 13-18.
