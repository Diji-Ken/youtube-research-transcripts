## 概要
Summary: 

Valon Rexhepi, Global VP Google Cloud Business Unit at Devoteam, shares how the European technology consulting company with 12,000 employees across 25 countries has embedded Gemini for Google Workspace into its daily operations. This adoption has transformed productivity across departments, saving time, improving workflows, and enhancing collaboration, all while maintaining security and trust in Google's ecosystem.

Challenge:

Devoteam needed a way to accelerate workflows and enhance productivity across diverse roles, including sales, marketing, HR, operations, and consulting. The challenge was to streamline repetitive tasks, such as generating content, responding to RFPs, and debugging code, while maintaining secure and seamless collaboration across their international teams.

Solution: 

The introduction of Gemini for Google Workspace allowed Devoteam to integrate AI into its operations. By selecting five key personas - sales, marketing, HR, operations, and consultants - they tested Gemini to automate tasks and improve efficiency. Features like AI-assisted content creation, job description generation, and code debugging were tailored to meet the needs of each role.

Results:

Significant time savings in creating marketing plans and assets.
70% reduction in RFP response time for sales teams.
Instant code debugging and suggestions for consultants.
Adoption of AI tools like GEMs, that can help anyone become an expert in any field.
Enhanced trust and security ensured by Google Workspace's infrastructure.
 
Interview highlights and key takeaways from our interview with Valon Rexhepi, Global VP Google Cloud Business Unit at Devoteam

""We have been using Google Workspace for over 15 years now. Google Workspace is really at the core of our culture.""
""We introduced Gemini to our organization because we do believe that AI is really transformative to every angle of our business.""
""As we love and trust Google Workspace for so many years, we know that our data is secure in the Cloud. Adding Gemini on top of that is just a natural extension of it, knowing that our data will remain our data.""
