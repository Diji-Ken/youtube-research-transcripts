## 概要
This installment of the @Google Cloud Security Showcase details how to protect devices with G Suite. G Suite has device management built into the platform. Although basic device management is on by default, you can also turn on advanced management for greater control. You can also install @Google Device Policy to further protect devices.

G Suite Security → https://goo.gle/2NIVRRp
