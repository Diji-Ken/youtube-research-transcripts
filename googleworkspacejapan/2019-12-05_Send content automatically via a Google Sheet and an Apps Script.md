## 概要
Thanks to the abundance of platforms that presently exist for creating content, so many people are able to create online white papers, ebooks, informational videos, or a tips and tricks blog, around different topics. In this episode, see why it can be handy to use a Google Form to help requestors receive these assets automatically by email using a Sheet’s Apps Script.
