## 概要
The New York City Department of Information Technology and Telecommunications brings mission-critical technologies to city agencies, making life for residents easier and safer. This includes creating apps and online services that New Yorkers can depend on, such as our newly launched Hurricane Zone Finder. Powered by Google Maps Engine, the Hurricane Zone Finder ensures that New Yorkers can get the information they need, when they need it most.
