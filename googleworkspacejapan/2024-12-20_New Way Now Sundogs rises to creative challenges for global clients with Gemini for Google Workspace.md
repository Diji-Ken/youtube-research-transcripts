## 概要
Summary: Sundogs, a creative performance consultancy, uses Google Workspace and Gemini for Google Workspace to help fuel its innovative approach to creative effectiveness in advertising. By embracing AI and data, Sundogs empowers its team to collaborate seamlessly, generate creative ideas, and deliver impactful results.

Challenge: As a small team, Sundogs needs agile tools that empower collaboration with colleagues and clients across time zones. The team also strives to fuse creativity, data, and AI to support productivity at scale, and wanted to embrace generative AI to improve the originality of its advertising campaigns.

Solution: Google Workspace has become the backbone of Sundogs' operations, enabling seamless collaboration and efficient project management with team members and clients around the world. With Gemini for Google Workspace, Sundogs has further elevated its creative processes by automating routine tasks, generating ideas, and accessing valuable insights to optimize ad creative.

Result: With Google Workspace and Gemini, Sundogs has improved its capacity for collaboration and increased employee productivity, while boosting creative impact with data-driven insights. Together, these tools have transformed Sundogs’ creative process, empowering its team to deliver exceptional results.

Now we're in a place where with the right prompts, we can do it in hours.
