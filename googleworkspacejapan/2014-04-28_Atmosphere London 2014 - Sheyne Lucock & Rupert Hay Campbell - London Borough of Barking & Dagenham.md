## 概要
Sheyne Lucock, General Inspector (IT) and Rupert Hay-Campbell, ICT & Information Governance Officer, London Borough of Barking & Dagenham, discuss the adoption of Chrome devices by the Council and how it helps them respond effectively to the challenges facing the public sector.
