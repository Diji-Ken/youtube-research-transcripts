## 概要
Facing the demands of a growing digital landscape, Verizon sought to empower its employees with enhanced personal productivity skills. Enter Laura Mae Martin, a renowned productivity coach with a proven track record of success at Google and beyond. Laura's training sessions, focused on time management, email mastery, and overall well-being, resonated deeply with Verizon's workforce. Aspi Havewala, AVP of Infrastructure and Digital Workplace at Verizon, will share how Laura's insights and practical tips, as outlined in her new book ""Uptime,"" have transformed productivity within the company.
