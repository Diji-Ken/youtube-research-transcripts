## 概要
This session will focus on security use cases that customers can solve with G Suite. After introducing new products, the majority of the session will involve demos across multiple product areas. But rather than a product walk-through, we'll attempt to solve real security issues such as: What do I do if I detect a phishing incident? Looks like a device is compromised, how do I go about remediating? How can I find out who is sharing what outside the organization?
