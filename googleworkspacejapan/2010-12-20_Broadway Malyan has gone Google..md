## 概要
CIO of Broadway Malyan - a UK-based, award-winning international practice of architects, urbanists and designers with + 500 employees across 13 offices - discusses their move from Microsoft Exchange 2010 to Google Apps for Business.

www.google.co.uk/gogoogle
