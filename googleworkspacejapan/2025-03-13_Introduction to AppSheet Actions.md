## 概要
This video provides a comprehensive introduction to AppSheet actions, a core feature for building powerful and intuitive apps. Learn how actions support a diverse set of distinct operations in an app, triggered by users or automations. Dive in to understand the four key action types and how they can transform your AppSheet creations! Discover practical examples of how to use actions to navigate views, manage data, connect to external apps, automate workflows and more.
