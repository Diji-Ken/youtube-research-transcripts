## 概要
Stuck troubleshooting an issue with the Google Apps Admin console? Learn to think the same way Google Support thinks about troubleshooting in this video.

Check out our 'How to think like a troubleshooter' video at http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0WZtnv9SZDQ for more information.
