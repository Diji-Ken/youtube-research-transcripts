## 概要
In this installment of the @Google Cloud Security Showcase, you’ll learn how to use Google Vault. This product lets you control how long your G Suite data is retained. Google Vault can also support e-discovery needs thanks to its advanced search and export capabilities. Note that Google Vault is meant for data retention, not backup—it does not make a copy of your data, but rather gives you more control over how it's stored.
