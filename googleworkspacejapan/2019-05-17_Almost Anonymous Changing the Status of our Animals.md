## 概要
For Endangered Species Day, Google Cloud is teaming up with World Wildlife Fund (WWF) and Netflix's “Our Planet” to raise awareness around animals that are at risk. 13 new animals—like the african wild dog, grey reef shark and cheetah—are joining the pack. Learn more at https://goo.gle/2wbktbv.
