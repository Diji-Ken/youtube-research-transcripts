## 概要
Amtrak is invested in making travel easier for its 31 million passengers every year. This means giving people accurate information about train locations and arrival times. Google Maps Engine is providing the technology behind Amtrak's newest online feature: an interactive train locator map that makes it easy for travelers to see where trains are and when they're getting to their destinations.
