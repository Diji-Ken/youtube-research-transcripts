## 概要
In this inspiring WHM/IWD 24 video, Amanda Taylor, VP of Infrastructure at FinQuery, shatters the myth of the lone wolf tech leader.  She reveals the power of supportive women in her life, like Christa Rouse who boosted her confidence, and Sua Jane, a data whiz who taught her to balance empathy with setting expectations.  But Amanda doesn't just talk about inspiration, she gets real.  Sharing a story about overcoming discomfort to advocate for her worth, she shows how open communication about compensation can empower women in tech.  And for the next generation, Amanda throws out the "choose one" narrative.  Watch and learn how she achieves career success while prioritizing a fulfilling personal life through smart work-life balance strategies.  This is your chance to be inspired and equipped to contribute to a more inclusive tech world for all.
