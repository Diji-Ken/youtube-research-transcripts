## 概要
Paul Clarke, Noam Bardin discusses with John Hagel what makes their companies innovative. Adopting certain core start-up elements and best practices is influencing employee behaviour and accelerating innovation. Feautring John Hagel (Co-founder, Center for the Edge), Paul Clarke (CIO, Ocado), Noam Bardin (Chief Wazer, Google).
