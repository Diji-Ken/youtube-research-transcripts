## 概要
Featured in this video: Dipesh Karki, Co-founder and CPTO, LenDenClub

Executive summary: LenDenClub is an online peer-to-peer lending marketplace that connects lenders and borrowers, operating in a highly regulated environment where security and collaboration are critical. As the company scaled, LenDenClub adopted Google Workspace to enable seamless collaboration while protecting sensitive financial and personal data through enterprise-grade security controls.

Building on this foundation, LenDenClub introduced Gemini to boost productivity across all departments, such as HR, Engineering and Branding. The company also leveraged Gemini to build internal reporting tools and optimize infrastructure costs. Together, Google Workspace with Gemini has transformed how LenDenClub works, enabling faster delivery, stronger security and a more empowered workforce.

Challenge:  As a fast-growing fintech platform handling sensitive customer data and financial transactions, LenDenClub faced increasing challenges around secure collaboration, productivity, and scalability. The company needed to protect PI data while enabling teams to move faster, reduce manual work, and improve efficiency across the organization. 

Solution: LenDenClub implemented Google Workspace to establish a secure, collaborative infrastructure supported by mobile device management, single sign-on, and multi-factor authentication. To further boost productivity, the company introduced Gemini across departments. HR teams used Gemini to standardize and accelerate hiring processes, engineering teams leveraged it for coding and mobile app development, and marketing teams adopted Veo to quickly produce multilingual videos. LenDenClub also built a custom reporting and analytics tool using the Gemini API and applied Gemini to infrastructure log analysis to optimize costs.

Results:  With Google Workspace and Gemini, LenDenClub has transformed how teams work across the organization. Recruitment timelines dropped from 60 days to just 23 days through AI-assisted screening and evaluation. Engineering teams now deliver end-to-end projects in 21 days instead of 45, while branding teams rapidly produce multilingual video content using Veo that once required extensive time and resources. Gemini-powered analytics and log processing have improved reporting and reduced infrastructure costs, enabling employees to focus on strategic, high-value work rather than repetitive tasks.
