## 概要
Summary: Travis Perkins PLC, the UK’s largest distributor of building materials, transformed its operations with a complete migration to Google Workspace. Ten years ago, the company made the strategic move to adopt Google Workspace to connect thousands of employees and streamline workflows. Today, they’re harnessing the full potential of Google’s tools, from Google Drive, Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Slides to enable seamless collaboration across their massive network. Plus by leveraging tools like AppSheet to digitize paper processes and making Google Sheets the organization’s powerhouse tool, they’ve unlocked smarter, more innovative ways of working. Google Workspace empowers their teams to collaborate efficiently, embrace creative solutions, and stay ahead in the fast-paced construction industry.

Challenge: Travis Perkins PLC faced challenges with outdated mailing systems that lacked collaborative features. The company aimed to modernize its construction approach and enhance collaborative work by adopting Google Workspace. They sought a modern platform that would enable efficient and effective collaboration across the organization. Today, Travis Perkins PLC utilizes a wide range of Google Workspace tools, including Google Drive, Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Slides for daily tasks. They also rely on Google Chat and Google Spaces for communication and collaboration, Looker Studio for data visualization and analysis, and AppSheet and AppScript for app development and automation. By implementing these tools, Travis Perkins has improved its collaborative culture and streamlined its operations

Solution: To overcome its challenges with outdated mailing systems and a lack of collaboration tools, Travis Perkins PLC adopted Google Workspace as a modern platform for efficient and effective collaboration. They sought to enhance their collaborative culture and streamline operations by implementing a suite of Google Workspace tools, including Google Drive, Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Slides, Google Chat, Google Spaces, Looker Studio, AppSheet, and AppScript. This migration enabled Travis Perkins PLC to modernize its approach to the construction industry and foster a more collaborative work environment.
