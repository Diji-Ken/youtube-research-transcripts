## 概要
Not sure what to do when you see suspicious activity alerts in G Suite? In this installment of the @Google Cloud Security Showcase, you’ll learn how to investigate an alert. You’ll start by learning how to determine which device is compromised and why, and then how to respond to threats proactively.
