## 概要
Featured in this video: Pebbles Sy, Chief Technology and Operations Officer, GCash

Executive summary: GCash is the Philippines’ number one finance super app and the country’s largest cashless ecosystem, with a mission to make financial services accessible to every Filipino. Operating at massive scale, GCash relies on Google Workspace as a core enabler of speed, alignment, and clarity across its teams. As a digital-first organization, GCash uses Google Workspace to collaborate seamlessly, make data-driven decisions, and move at the pace its customers expect.

Building on this foundation, GCash adopted Gemini and NotebookLM to reshape how work gets done across the organization. Teams now use Gemini to accelerate research, summarize meetings, generate reports, and automate repetitive tasks, while NotebookLM supports product design and architectural decision-making by synthesizing documents, roadmaps, and research into shared knowledge. Research cycles that once took five days are now completed in half a day, and adoption has been strong, with 89% of employees actively using Gemini. Together, Google Workspace, Gemini, and NotebookLM are enabling GCash to work faster, think more clearly, and deliver greater value to customers at scale.

Challenge: As the largest cashless ecosystem in the Philippines, GCash needed to operate with exceptional speed, coordination, and clarity across teams. Manual research, complex documentation, and alignment-heavy decision-making slowed progress, making it harder to scale innovation and respond quickly to customer needs in a fast-moving financial landscape.

Solution: GCash standardized collaboration on Google Workspace to support real-time teamwork and data-driven decision-making. To further accelerate execution, the company adopted Gemini and NotebookLM as part of its everyday workflow. Gemini helps teams shorten research cycles, summarize information, and automate routine tasks, while NotebookLM enables architects and product leaders to quickly understand dependencies, risks, and trade-offs, and even generate early drafts of architecture decision records. These AI tools are embedded directly into the Workspace environment, making them easy to adopt and use at scale.

Results: With Google Workspace, Gemini, and NotebookLM, GCash has dramatically accelerated how teams research, design, and build for users. Research and alignment efforts have been reduced from days to hours, and 89% of employees now use Gemini as part of their daily toolkit. By turning complexity into clarity and freeing teams from repetitive work, GCash is enabling employees to focus on creativity, ideation, and solving real customer problems. The result is faster innovation, stronger alignment, and work that more directly moves the needle for GCash’s customers, shaping a future of work powered by AI and driven by purpose.
