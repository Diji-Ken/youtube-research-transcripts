## 概要
Every good idea starts as a spreadsheet, and every great idea eventually outgrows that spreadsheet. In this video, Joanna recaps how Eric Koleda and Charles Maxson progressively enhance their spreadsheets and grow them into fully functional applications. 

Explore how to apply Forms, Apps Script, and even App Maker to these spreadsheets, and get a sneak peak into a real-world example from within Google to illustrate these points.
