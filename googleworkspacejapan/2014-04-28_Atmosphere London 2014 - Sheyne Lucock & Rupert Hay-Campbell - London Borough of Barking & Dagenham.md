## 概要
Sheyne Lucock, General Inspector (IT), and Rupert Hay-Campbell, ICT & Information Governance Officer, London Borough of Barking & Dagenham, discuss the transformational impact of embracing new technologies and outline their targets for making even greater efficiencies.
