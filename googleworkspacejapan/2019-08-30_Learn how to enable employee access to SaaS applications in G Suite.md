## 概要
Not sure how to grant employees access to SaaS applications in G Suite? SaaS applications often support a variety of authentication protocols. This installment of the @Google Cloud Security Showcase will walk you through how to configure single sign-on using SAML-based applications and HeldaP-based applications.
