【Lime Honey Chicken Wings】
What to Eat Tonight? AI 852 Cooking Guide

Tonight, you can try cooking this: Quick and Delicious Recipe for 【Lime Honey Chicken Wings】

〔Ingredients Needed〕： Chicken wings, Lime, Honey, Salt, Pepper, Garlic, Soy sauce.

〔Step 1〕Thaw the chicken wings in light saltwater. (As for why you should use light saltwater, there's a tip at the end of the video that explains it.) After thawing, pat them dry with a paper towel.  
〔Step 2〕Cut the lime in half and squeeze out the juice. Peel and chop the garlic.  
〔Step 3〕Marinate the chicken wings by adding salt, pepper, soy sauce, garlic, lime juice, and honey. Mix well, cover with cling wrap, and refrigerate for two hours.  
〔Step 4〕Bake at 180°C (356°F) for 20 minutes in an oven or convection oven (if necessary, flip the chicken wings halfway).

Done！

〔Taste Review〕 The crispy baked chicken wings paired with tangy lime juice and the sweetness of honey make this dish both refreshing and convenient!  
〔Recommendation〕 ★★★★☆

〔Tip〕 Thawing in light saltwater can help reduce the freezer taste in frozen meat.
